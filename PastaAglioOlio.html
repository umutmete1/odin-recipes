Pasta Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 large garlic cloves, thinly sliced crosswise
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • Salt
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Recipe

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the spaghetti until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking water.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large, deep skillet, heat the olive oil. Add the garlic and crushed red pepper and cook over moderately high heat, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is golden, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the spaghetti to the skillet along with the reserved cooking water and cook, tossing, until the water is nearly absorbed, about 2 minutes.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat, add the parsley and toss. Season the pasta with salt and transfer to bowls. Sprinkle with the cheese and serve.
